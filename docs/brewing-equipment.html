Brewing Tips & Usage Guide

Water Temperature

Use water between 195-205°F (90-96°C) for optimal extraction. Too hot water can over-extract and create bitter flavors.

Coffee Ratio

Use a 1:15 to 1:17 coffee-to-water ratio. Start with 1:16 (1g coffee to 16g water) and adjust to taste.

Brewing Time

Different methods require different times: Pour-over (3-4 min), French Press (4 min), Espresso (25-30 sec).

Grind Size

Match grind size to brewing method: Fine for espresso, medium for pour-over, coarse for French press.
